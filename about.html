About Me

Electronics Engineer

I am a first-year Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering student based in Pune, dedicated to mastering the intersection of hardware and software. My technical journey is driven by a deep curiosity for how electronic systems power the modern world, leading me to develop a strong foundation in circuit analysis, logic gates, and the mechanics of physics.

Beyond the classroom, I am an active problem-solver who enjoys bringing theoretical concepts to life through hands-on projects, such as building smart assistive devices with Arduino or designing water level detectors. I am proficient in programming with C and Python, using these tools to bridge the gap between abstract code and physical components. My goal is to leverage my engineering expertise to build reliable, high-impact technical solutions that push the boundaries of modern communication and electronics.
